# Adaptive Inquiry And Independence

Track `claim_or_action`, known/unknown/N/A, live interpretations, evidence for/against, highest-value discriminating observation, validity/risk limits, and disposition. Start anywhere and loop only when the observation could change the claim, action, or risk.

Use `context-closure-and-human-input.md` before commitment. The self-grill retrieves task background and decision-changing facts; adaptive inquiry then updates interpretations as evidence arrives. Neither authorizes broad questioning or mandatory linear phases.

Define evidence universe, sampling, exclusions, coverage, provenance, and access constraints. Combine systematic/random coverage with risk, transition, boundary, influence, failure, and ordinary-success cases. Preserve untouched confirmation for generalizing claims and include an expectation-independent control for consequential claims.

An `IndependenceSpec` records reviewer initial context; owner information withheld until precommitment; evidence universe and reviewer sampling; overlap ceiling; source/tool/model diversity; holdout and contamination; authority; and conflicts of interest.

Use `continue_with_replan`, `narrow_or_withhold`, `block_action`, or `stop_execution`. Blocking an effect does not block safe diagnostics. Deadlines never convert missing evidence into approval.
