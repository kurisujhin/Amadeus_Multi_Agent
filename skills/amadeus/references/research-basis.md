# Research Basis And Limits

These peer-reviewed human studies motivate design hypotheses; they do not prove an LLM-agent mechanism, optimal topology, or universal cadence.

## Insight And Incubation

- Sio and Ormerod (2009), meta-analysis: incubation had an overall benefit moderated by task type, preparation, interval activity, and cognitive demand. DOI: https://doi.org/10.1037/a0014212
- Baird et al. (2012): a low-demand task associated with more mind wandering improved later performance on previously encountered divergent-thinking items, not a general license for idle time. DOI: https://doi.org/10.1177/0956797612446024
- Gilhooly (2016), review: unconscious work, intermittent conscious work, beneficial forgetting, spreading activation, and goal activation remain overlapping explanations rather than one settled mechanism. DOI: https://doi.org/10.3389/fpsyg.2016.01075
- Jung-Beeman et al. (2004): verbal insight showed pre-solution and right anterior temporal neural correlates consistent with remote semantic integration. DOI: https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pbio.0020097
- Shen et al. (2016), neuroimaging meta-analysis: neural findings vary substantially by insight task, cautioning against a universal insight circuit. DOI: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.ijpsycho.2016.10.001
- Beaty et al. (2015): creative idea production involved dynamic coupling between default and executive-control networks, supporting interaction between spontaneous and controlled processes rather than a rigid serial law. DOI: https://doi.org/10.1038/srep10964
- Salvi et al. (2016): self-reported insight solutions were more accurate on average than analytic solutions across their tasks. DOI: https://doi.org/10.1080/13546783.2016.1141798
- Danek and Wiley (2017): incorrect solutions can also produce Aha experiences; phenomenology is not a validity certificate. DOI: https://doi.org/10.3389/fpsyg.2016.02077

Engineering hypothesis: permit bounded, optional generation or incubation; capture sudden ideas without demanding a causal story; then separate generation from falsification, confirmation, and independent review. “From nowhere” describes conscious access, not evidence of causeless generation.

## Team And Control Design

- Keiser (2022): unnecessary shared-model overlap can overload teams; needed overlap depends on task interdependence. DOI: https://doi.org/10.1108/TPM-07-2021-0051
- Mirani (2007): task partitioning and integration mechanisms jointly manage distributed interdependence. DOI: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.im.2006.12.001
- Cummings and Kiesler (2007): broader collaboration increases coordination cost; division of responsibility and knowledge-transfer coordination matter. DOI: https://doi.org/10.1016/j.respol.2007.09.001
- Tannenbaum and Cerasoli (2013), meta-analysis: structured debriefs improved effectiveness on average, favoring bounded learning over constant interruption. PMID: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/23516804/
- Al-Khafaji et al. (2022), systematic review: checklist effects were mixed and task-oriented components more often aligned with error reduction than cognitive prompts alone. DOI: https://doi.org/10.1136/bmjopen-2021-058219

The executor-to-observer feedback protocol is a workflow-engineering hypothesis, not established by the human insight literature. V4 originally tested that hypothesis relative to v3; v5 promotion compares the unchanged v4 snapshot with the exact v5 candidate on hidden transfers and non-examples. Unresolved empirical questions include optimal agent count, interval, observer cadence, model diversity, transfer sample size, and latency-quality frontier.
